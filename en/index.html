Areas of action

The project is divided into two main areas of action:

  • Development of an advanced prompt engineering technique: This area focuses on the creation of specific and optimized prompts for large-scale AI models (72B parameters), in order to improve the accuracy in identifying printing techniques.
  • Development and training of a deep learning model: This area involves the creation and training of a deep learning model specifically designed to recognize printing techniques through the visual analysis of images.

Project targets

  • Initial Phase: Automatically identify pre-photographic printing technologies (e.g. lithography, engraving, woodcut, etching, etc.) through automated visual analysis.
  • Next Step: Extend the functionality to recognize photographic printing techniques (e.g. albumin, silver gelatin, cyanotypes, etc.).

Final applications

Provide effective assistance for:

  • Digital archiving of art collections.
  • Restoration of graphic works through a better understanding of the original techniques used.
